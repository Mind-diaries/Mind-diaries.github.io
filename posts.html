Fuckall Happiness!

You are not happy as you pretend, but miserable!

Posted by Hypocritus Dominus on August 10, 2021

People read this post
What is happiness?, It’s the moment before you need more happiness.
- Don Draper in Mad Men

Welcome to the 21st Century peeps, pursuing happiness is the new ‘black’. We are collecting moments like shells at the beach. Its futile.

Yes, I am referring to the devil-bred social media. It is a façade. There is nothing that portrays modern existential emptiness quite like the person travelling the world while constantly posting pictures of restaurants and landmarks on social media. In trying to be happier – better – than others, this person risks alienating himself from making genuine connections with his peers. I’m not saying being happy is bad. Far from it. Live it up.

No one is to blame

We are being victimised by the ever-powerful Social media, that manipulates our moods with its fuckall News Feed algorithms, sending us all on a fuckall roller-coaster ride. Mundane life experiences getting airbrushed out of the frame, It’s as though being unhappy appears to be a moral fault: as if we didn’t work hard enough or the worst,
lack self-esteem.

We are allowing ourselves to be manipulated, leaving ourselves open to poor judgement, everlasting loneliness and ironically, an abiding sadness. 22 per cent of millennials saying they feel depressed at all times, is not a joke. This, surely, is not the kind of ‘happiness’ we want to pursue.

Sadness is fundamental in being a human. Afterall, life would not be worth living if it floated only between peak experiences. So its high time, we recondition ourselves: not to want but to be content in all feelings. To be unbothered by the daily humdrums of so called ‘life’.

The awakening!

Laugh, Love, Cry, Smash, Fuck. Ride your emotions. Feel it all!
You hold the key to the gates of Eden. That’s the secret being constantly spilled by our ageless saints throughout history.

Fuck its 2:00 AM, let's wrap it up here. Its time for me to dive into my cozy bed, pick up my ‘smart’phone and lose myself in the endless ocean of ‘feeds’, till sunrise.

Good night and Sweet dreams.
